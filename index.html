A Tribute to LCD Soundsystem

Bio

LCD Soundsystem was formed in 2002 by lead singer James Murphy.
Their first single was Losing My Edge, and there were a few more
following up to their first album being nominated for a Grammy.

This was followed up by Sound Of Silver also being nominated, and,
after their reunion 10 years later, they won a grammy for their single
"Tonite".
